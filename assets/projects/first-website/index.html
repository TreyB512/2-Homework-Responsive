Welcome to My website!

Hello, and welcome to my website! My name is Cleo Brooks III and I am a Junior at Texas State University. I am a mass communications digital media innovation major with a minor in Computer science. I started my college career at Texas State and plan to finish here as well in the upcoming fall of 2019. Through my time at Texas State, I’ve taken classes in many different fields. This includes physical therapy studies, radiational therapy, and exercise and sports management. After going through all these different programs, I’m glad I can say I found the best one in the digital media.

This is my 3rd year now at Texas State and I'm loving every bit. I choose my major and minor in hopes of landing a Web developing job near Austin. I’m still just getting my feet wet in the world of coding, but with more learning and practicing there’s not limits in what could be done.

When I was younger I dealt with multiple surgeries due to a broken femur from playing city league football. From this unlucky injury. I attended physical therapy for a couple of months. It was bumpy at first being I was a lazy 12-year-old kid at the time, but the wonderful therapist I worked with through the process made everything worth it. In turn, I choose Texas State Physical Therapy program and began taking classes my spring freshman year. Everything was going great, then around my Sophomore year I ended up changing my major to radiation therapy after realizing I did not want to go to school an extra four years for PT school. I switched to radiation therapy major for a semester when afterwards I realized how much I really missed technology and got introduced to coding by a friend. About a week later I discovered the new digital media program Texas state just added as a major. So here I am now. At the end of my Junior year with no regrets on the decision I made.
